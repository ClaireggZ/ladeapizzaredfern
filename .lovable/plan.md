

# LA DEA Pizza & Dolci Website

A mobile-first Italian pizzeria website with **bold red background** (#C41E3A) and **white text**, designed for QR code access.

---

## Branding & Design

- **Primary Color:** Rich Cardinal Red (#C41E3A) matching the logo
- **Text:** White throughout for high contrast
- **Theme:** Roman goddess / Italian heritage aesthetic
- **Typography:** Elegant serif fonts for classical feel
- **Assets:** LA DEA logo + 3 pizza photos provided

---

## Page 1: Home (Brand Introduction)

- Large LA DEA logo prominently displayed
- Hero section with taglines "Creato a Roma" • "Cibo Per Gli Dei"
- Brief brand story celebrating the Roman goddess theme
- Beautiful pizza photography showcase
- Clear navigation to Menu and Contact pages
